What is ACTI?

ACTI (Awakening Consciousness Type Indicator) is a multi-dimensional diagnostic framework designed to clarify how people operate and grow.

ACTI is not astrology. It is not a personality label. It is not a clinical diagnosis.

ACTI focuses on operating patterns and stages — not fixed identities.

ACTI models how consciousness works — not how personalities look.

ACTI observes human behavior through four interrelated dimensions. These dimensions describe how awareness is functioning now — not who you are supposed to be.

The value of ACTI lies not in the theories themselves, but in how they are synthesized into a usable map of consciousness.

27 operating patterns of human consciousness

ACTI types do not describe who you are. They describe how your awareness tends to operate — and how that operation can evolve.

ACTI types are not destinations. They are operating patterns — and every pattern can evolve.

Six stages of how consciousness relates to life

ACTI levels do not measure how “good” or “bad” someone is. They describe from which stage awareness is currently operating.

Consciousness levels are not ranks. They are operating stages. People may move between levels over time — through experience, pressure, insight, or deliberate change. ACTI shows where awareness is centered now, not where it should be.

A model refined through years of real human transformation

ACTI was created through over a decade of applied psychological work and real-world observation of human change. Developed in Japan, ACTI integrates analytical structure with deep introspective insight — without reducing people to labels.

ACTI does not rely on AI to decide results. AI is used only to support interpretation and language — helping users understand their results with clarity and precision.
